Recipe

Detox Smoothie Bowl

Time taken: 12 mins

Ingredients

  • Banana
  • Spinach
  • Yogurt
  • Microgreens

Procedure

  1. Blend banana, spinach, yogurt.
  2. Pour into bowl.
  3. Top with seeds and microgreens.
  4. Serve chilled.
