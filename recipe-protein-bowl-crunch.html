Recipe

Protein Bowl Crunch

Time taken: 20 mins

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cooked rice/quinoa
  • Roasted veggies
  • Paneer/chicken
  • Microgreens

Procedure

  1. Build base of grain and protein.
  2. Add warm veggies.
  3. Top with fresh microgreens.
  4. Finish with lemon dressing.
