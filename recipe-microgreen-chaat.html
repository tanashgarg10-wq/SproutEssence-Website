Recipe

Microgreen Chaat

Time taken: 15 mins

Ingredients

  • Boiled potato
  • Chickpeas
  • Onion
  • Microgreens

Procedure

  1. Combine potato and chickpeas.
  2. Add onion, masala and chutney.
  3. Top with microgreens.
  4. Serve fresh.
