About my journey

A path shaped by nutrition, diverse industries, and a return to purpose.

My journey began in the field of nutrition, where I formally trained as a nutritionist and built a strong foundation in health, wellness, and mindful eating. I went on to work closely with hospitals for nearly two years, including valuable experience at Manipal Hospital, where I gained hands-on exposure to clinical nutrition and patient-centered care.

Curiosity and a desire to explore diverse professional landscapes then led me beyond healthcare. I spent several years in the IT sector, gaining insights into structured systems, operations, and problem-solving in a fast-paced corporate environment. Along the way, I also worked in the real estate sector, which further strengthened my understanding of business dynamics, people management, and long-term planning.

Despite these varied experiences, my passion for nutrition and clean, conscious food never faded. Over time, this passion naturally evolved into a deep interest in microgreens—nutrient-dense, sustainable, and powerful in their impact on everyday health. Today, that passion has come full circle, bringing me to where I am now: combining my background in nutrition with my love for growing fresh, wholesome food, and sharing it with others in its purest form.

This journey, shaped by learning, exploration, and purpose, continues to guide everything I do.
