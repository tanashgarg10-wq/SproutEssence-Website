About my journey

A path shaped by nutrition, diverse industries, and a return to purpose.

My journey began in nutrition, where I formally trained and built a strong foundation in health and mindful eating. I worked closely with hospitals, including Manipal Hospital, gaining hands-on clinical experience.

I later explored IT and real estate, gaining valuable insights into operations, business systems, and long-term planning.

Over time, my passion for clean, conscious food evolved into microgreens — nutrient-dense, sustainable, and powerful in everyday health. Today, Sprout Essence blends my nutrition background with my love for growing fresh, wholesome food.
