Recipe

Morning Green Toast

Time taken: 10 mins

Ingredients

  • 2 slices wholegrain bread
  • Hung curd or cream cheese
  • Cucumber slices
  • Microgreens handful

Procedure

  1. Toast the bread.
  2. Spread hung curd.
  3. Add cucumber and top with microgreens.
  4. Serve immediately.
